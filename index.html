About Us

 

 


Humanity has thrived for aeons because of its harmonious bond with nature - A bond unfazed for long. But off late, we have witnessed a contamination of our beloved ambience with an eccentric lifestyle over the past century. Our insatiable desire to advance at the expense of nature has debilitated our bond with nature.


We, as students discovered that an issue so fundamental to our existence does not garner the required attention in the formative years of an individual.


So, come this March of 2015, as the new year ascends, we relive that divine bond, rejuvenate the surroundings with awareness, repay in full and in care: our attention to mother earth, revise our obligations for a greener world and respire in the festivity of NATURE.

Campus Ambassadors

  • What We Look For


    The Campus Ambassadorship Program by Catharsis'15 is a nationwide programme which exposes the students nationwide from various colleges as efficient managers and creative leaders and provides an opportunity to be a part of Catharsis 2015. Members not only acquire leadership/ entrepreneurial skills but also gain a social streak with a good character. We call upon our ambassadors to educate their constituents about the powerful role of socio-initiatives.

  • Criteria


    All the college/university students from age group (17-25) holding some position of responsibility in their college and are capable of appreciably representing the views and policies of an ambassador during campaigns are eligible to apply. Applicant must be consistent to work efficiently during his/her tenure. A maximum of 2 ambassadors Would be selected from each college.

  • Role


    We look for students who are leaders in their campus, leaders who can inspire their peers, collaborate with administrators and organize campus events to leave impact. It's a chance to develop and grow as an individual while adding an incredible line to your resume. This is for those who are having keen interest in updating people about various Catharsis'15 events through public meets, announcements, notices, social-media, web pages, etc.
